ABOUT VILA TOPI

Vila Topi More
than a stay

Vila Topi offers a garden, as well as accommodation with a kitchen in Vlorë, 1.3 km from Vjetër Beach. The apartment provides rooms with air conditioning, free private parking and free WiFi.

The apartment complex features some units that include garden views, and every unit comes with a private bathroom and a wardrobe.
